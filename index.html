Welcome To Imperial Hotel

Imperial Hotel is situated on the waterfront opposite the Royal Palace and the Old Town, 1 km from Central Station. It offers fine dining, a free luxury spa and free Wi-Fi access. Imperial Hotel is soundproofed, air-conditioned rooms have an LCD TV with satellite channels. Rooms also offer courtyard, city or waterfront views.
